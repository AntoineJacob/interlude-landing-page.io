What is Interlude

Interlude is a social media where people can share and discover music with everyone depending their musical tastes.

Create your profile, add the kind of music you like and find a new song that you will like !

Post music

Use Interlude to post your music thanks to youtube/spotify link.

People can react with like and comment your post.

The feed

On interlude, we have a feed with all the users posts and a other feed more personal, depending your musical taste. You can like and comment each post.

Customize your profile

Add your personal taste thanks to our "#" system and find people with the same taste than you

Who created Interlude ?

Interlude was created by Maxence Thibault and Antoine Jacob as part of their end-of-year portfolio project at Holberton School Laval.

"We wanted to create a place where anyone could discuss, discover or introduce other people to music.

Building on the knowledge we had acquired in our first year, we developed Interlude."
