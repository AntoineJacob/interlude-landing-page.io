What is Interlude

Interlude is a social media where people can share and discover music with everyone depending their musical tastes.

First, register, then add your musical tastes & follow people to have a personalized feed and finally scroll to find new songs that you like !

Post music

Use Interlude to make posts, share music thanks to youtube or spotify links and add a caption.

Once it's posted, people can interact with your post by liking and commenting.

The feed

On interlude, we have a personalized feed.

It will display your own posts and all the posts of the people you are following.

As explained below, we use "#" to have a feed closer to your tastes

Customize your profile

You have the possibility to add a profile picture and a bio like any other social media.

We added a special element called "My musical tastes" where you can write about the genre or artists you like.

And if you put a "#" before, all posts containing the same "#" in their caption will automatically be added to your feed !

Who created Interlude ?

Interlude was created by Maxence Thibault and Antoine Jacob as part of their end-of-year portfolio project at Holberton School Laval.

"We wanted to create a place where anyone can discuss, discover and introduce music to anyone !.

Building on the knowledge we had acquired in our first year, we developed Interlude."
